Who we are

The New Zealand Campus of Innovation & Sport has a unique style when it comes to hospitality. With over 350 accommodation rooms, multiple lecture theatres, conference and breakout rooms and beautiful food on these beautiful park-like grounds.

NZCIS is the perfect venue for business or training groups.

We are conveniently located across the road from the Heretaunga train station for easy access to the rest of the Wellington region.

Why us?

NZCIS Has so much to offer. Some of the things people love us for are:

  • One stop Accommodation/Dining/Conferencing
  • 100m from Heretaunga Railway Station
  • 30 minutes drive to Wellington CBD
  • 15 minutes Lower Hutt CBD
  • Free onsite parking
  • Park like surrounds
